Tincture

Echinacea
Immunity
Tincture

A powerful extract made from echinacea root to strengthen the immune system and help ward off colds.

Benefits

  • Boosts immunity
  • Supports recovery from illness
  • Reduces severity of cold symptoms

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh or dried echinacea root, chopped
  • 2 cups high-proof alcohol (vodka or brandy)

Directions

  1. Place echinacea root into a glass jar.
  2. Cover completely with alcohol and seal tightly.
  3. Store in a cool, dark place for 4–6 weeks, shaking occasionally.
  4. Strain and pour into a dropper bottle. Take 10–20 drops diluted in water daily.
