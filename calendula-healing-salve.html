Salve

Calendula
Healing Salve

A gentle skin salve infused with calendula, known for its skin-soothing and anti-inflammatory properties.

Benefits

  • Promotes healing of cuts, scrapes, and burns
  • Reduces skin irritation
  • Moisturizes dry or chapped skin

Ingredients

  • 1 cup calendula-infused oil
  • 1 oz beeswax
  • 5 drops lavender essential oil (optional)

Directions

  1. Melt beeswax in a double boiler.
  2. Stir in calendula oil until combined.
  3. Add lavender essential oil if desired.
  4. Pour into tins or jars and allow to cool.
  5. Apply to affected skin as needed.
